My Journey


  My journey as a photographer started in 2017 when I received an iPhone X as a Christmas gift. I began taking pictures whenever I could, taking dozens every single day. I watched countless videos on how to take photographs and edit them on my iPhone. I began getting results I was pleased with, especially considering how little experience I had with the topic.



photos taken with my iPhone X


I then got into Astrophotography with my parents' old point-and-shoot camera with help from friends on Twitter. I quickly got the hang of it but immediately felt limited by the camera.



a photo taken on my parents' camera


Later, I purchased a Sony a6000 in December of 2019.



the first photograph taken with my Sony a6000


I did mainly astrophotography with it for a while, but also dabbled in other areas of photography.



photos taken during the first months with my new camera
